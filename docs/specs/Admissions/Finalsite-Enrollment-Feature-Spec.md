# Feature Spec: Finalsite Enrollment (Admissions & CRM)

## 1. Overview
Finalsite Enrollment is the flagship enrollment management product from Finalsite, created from the acquisition of SchoolAdmin in 2021. It is a unified platform for marketing, recruitment, admissions, and enrollment for schools.

## 2. Feature List
### Post-Acquisition Integration
- **Unified Family Journey:** Connects the school's public website (Finalsite CMS) directly to the admissions funnel.
- **Data Flow:** Automatic synchronization between Finalsite's marketing assets and the enrollment system.
- **Marketing Integration:** Leverage digital ads and SEO to drive prospective families into the inquiry system.

### Admissions & Recruitment
- **Inquiry Management:** Customizable inquiry forms to track prospective students.
- **Dynamic Checklists:** Automated checklists for parents to see missing application steps.
- **Prospect CRM:** Advanced tools to nurture leads and manage "shadow days" and school tours.
- **Reporting:** Powerful admissions funnel reports (Inquiry → Applicant → Enrolled).

### Enrollment & Contract Management
- **Digital Contracts:** Generate and manage enrollment contracts with electronic signatures.
- **Parent Portal:** A single secure login for families to pay application fees and tuition.
- **Continuous Enrollment:** Streamlined re-enrollment for returning students without new contracts.

## 3. Pricing
- **Custom Quoting:** Finalsite Enrollment is an enterprise-grade product; pricing is based on school size, student volume, and feature modules.
- **Standard Support:** Unlimited access to customer support is typically included in all packages.

## 4. Integration Patterns
- **Finalsite CMS:** Deep integration for a unified website and enrollment experience.
- **SIS Connectivity:** Native integrations with Blackbaud, PowerSchool, and Infinite Campus (via GG4L).
- **API Access:** Robust REST API for custom data flows into other school systems.
- **Data Scrubbing:** Built-in tools for bulk data uploads with duplicate matching.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Consolidation Friction:** As the product evolves from "SchoolAdmin" to "Finalsite Enrollment," some older features may be deprecated or reorganized.
- **Higher Cost:** This is a high-end product designed for private and independent schools; price may be prohibitive for smaller institutions.
- **Module-Based Costs:** Additional features (like tuition billing or advanced marketing) may require separate subscriptions.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Finalsite vs. Blackbaud:** Finalsite is often preferred for its "Marketing-First" approach, helping schools attract new students.
- **Holistic Approach:** Best for schools that want one vendor for their website, communications, and admissions.
- **The Winner:** Best for private/independent schools that need a professional, automated admissions funnel to drive enrollment numbers and match it with a world-class public website.
