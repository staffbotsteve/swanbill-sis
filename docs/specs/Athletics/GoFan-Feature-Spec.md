# GoFan Feature Spec: Digital Ticketing for Athletics

## 1. Overview
GoFan is the leading digital ticketing platform for high school sports, serving thousands of schools and state associations. It is a part of the **PlayOn Sports** ecosystem, which also includes the NFHS Network and MaxPreps.

## 2. Core Features
### Mobile-First Ticketing
- **Digital Passes:** Mobile-only tickets; no printing or cash at the gate.
- **Season Passes:** Bundled tickets for a specific sport or academic year.
- **Reserved Seating:** Custom seat maps for gymnasiums and stadiums.
- **Ticket Transfer:** Easily send tickets to family/friends via text or email.

### Gate Management
- **Tap-to-Redeem:** Fast validation on fan devices; no scanning equipment required.
- **Offline Redemption:** QR code scanning for venues with poor Wi-Fi/cellular.
- **GoFan Box Office:** iOS device + card reader for on-site credit/debit and Apple/Google Pay.
- **Self-Check-in:** Option for fans to check themselves in at certain events.

### Reporting & Finance
- **Real-time Sales:** Track ticket counts and revenue in the PlayOn HQ dashboard.
- **Weekly Payouts:** Automatic ACH direct deposits or checks.
- **Consolidated Data:** Unified reporting across online, mobile, and on-site sales.

### School Branding
- **Custom Event Pages:** Branded portal for all school activities (athletics, arts, dances).

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **Core Platform:** Free for schools/districts. (Funded by attendee convenience fees).
- **Convenience Fees:**
    - Tickets ≤ $10: $1.00 per ticket.
    - Tickets > $10: $1.00 + 5% of face value.
- **Processing Fees:** GoFan covers all credit card processing fees for schools.

## 4. Integrations
- **PlayOn HQ:** Unified management for GoFan, NFHS Network, and MaxPreps.
- **MaxPreps:** Syncs scores and schedules to GoFan for automated event creation.
- **Identity:** Integrates with Google and Microsoft for staff access.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Digital Only:** Resistance from parents/seniors who prefer cash or printed tickets (mitigated by new Box Office tools).
- **Service Fees:** Can be perceived as high for very low-priced tickets ($1 fee on a $3 student ticket).
- **App Required:** While web-based purchase is possible, the best experience is through the GoFan app.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rival:** HomeTown Ticketing (more flexible with custom branding and printed tickets).
- **Edge:** Deepest integration with state high school athletic associations and the NFHS Network for live streaming.
- **API Status:** Private/Limited access; primarily operates within the PlayOn HQ ecosystem.
