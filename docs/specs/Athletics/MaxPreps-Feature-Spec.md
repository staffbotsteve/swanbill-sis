# MaxPreps Feature Spec: Athletic Stats & Recruiting

## 1. Overview
MaxPreps is the leading source for high school sports scores, stats, and rankings. It provides a platform for coaches, athletes, and fans to track performance and gain national visibility. It is now a part of the **PlayOn Sports** ecosystem, which also includes GoFan and the NFHS Network.

## 2. Core Features
### Stats & Score Tracking
- **National Database:** Scores and stats for 29 sports across Varsity, JV, and Freshman levels.
- **Automated Stats (NFHS Sync):** Game footage captured by NFHS Network cameras can be automatically broken down into team/player stats.
- **MaxStats App:** Interactive iPad scorekeeping for basketball that uploads directly to the site.
- **Roster & Schedule Management:** Coaches can manage team details and game schedules from their admin accounts.

### Recruiting & Exposure
- **Athlete Profiles:** Digital profiles highlighting stats, height, weight, photos, and video highlights.
- **Verified Metrics:** Partners with services like Elite 11 to showcase verified quarterback data to recruiters.
- **Rankings & News:** National and state rankings for teams and top recruits.
- **Video Highlights:** Auto-generated post-game recap videos for home, away, and neutral perspectives.

### Coaching Tools (MaxPreps Advantage)
- **Advanced Breakdowns:** Professional-grade video analysis and tendency reports (for 11 sports).
- **Unlimited Video Storage:** Cloud-based storage for game and scout film.
- **Film Exchange:** Easily share game footage with other coaches for scouting.
- **Digital Playbooks:** Tools to create and share tactical plays with the team.

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **Standard Membership:** Free for coaches, athletes, and fans.
- **MaxPreps Advantage (All Access):** Free for all schools that stream on the NFHS Network.
- **Advanced Breakdowns Package:** ~$600 per team / season (Includes stats and video analysis).
- **Fan Subscriptions (NFHS Network):** Fans pay a monthly/annual fee to watch live games.

## 4. Integrations
- **NFHS Network:** Automated video capture and stat generation from live-streamed games.
- **GoFan:** Scores and schedules sync with GoFan for automated ticketing event creation.
- **Recruiting Partners:** NCSA and BeRecruited for deeper athlete marketing.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Data Inaccuracy:** Scores and stats are only as good as the coaches who enter them; inconsistent reporting can be an issue.
- **Privacy:** Managing athlete profiles and removing data for former students can be a manual process.
- **Consolidation:** The move to the PlayOn HQ dashboard has required a learning curve for veteran coaches.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rival:** Hudl (more advanced video tools), Scorebook Live (SBLive).
- **Edge:** The "official" source for state associations; the massive volume of traffic (millions of users) provides the best "exposure" for student-athletes.
- **API Status:** Limited public API; data is primarily consumed through the website, app, and PlayOn HQ integrations.
