# FinalForms Feature Spec: Athletic Compliance

## 1. Overview
FinalForms is a specialized athletic management software designed for K-12 school districts. It focuses on **compliance**, **digital forms**, and **student safety**, aiming to eliminate paperwork and reduce liability for athletic departments.

## 2. Core Features
### Digital Registration & Forms
- **Year-over-Year Data:** Parents register once; data is saved season-to-season.
- **Customizable Forms:** Supports state, district, and department-required forms (e.g., Sudden Cardiac Arrest Awareness).
- **Electronic Signatures:** Parents and students can sign all forms online.
- **ADA Compliance:** Ensures an inclusive and accessible experience for all users.

### Compliance & Safety Tracking
- **Physical Management:** Tracks physical exam expiration dates; parents can upload scans/photos.
- **Real-time Eligibility:** Dashboard for ADs and coaches to see who is "cleared" for participation.
- **Automated Alerts:** Sends reminders to parents for missing signatures, expiring physicals, and eligibility issues.
- **Emergency Medical Cards:** Coaches have instant mobile access to student health alerts and emergency contacts.

### Communication & Reporting
- **Real-time Alerts:** Notifies staff, parents, and athletes about missed practices, injury updates, and concussion protocols.
- **Custom Reports:** Generate rosters, tournament entry forms, and eligibility reports automatically.
- **Activity Log:** Time-stamped record of all registration and status changes for accountability.

### Equipment & Payments
- **Equipment Management:** Track items assigned to students, billing for lost items, and reconditioning dates.
- **Fee Collection:** Integration with school payment systems for sports fees and payment plans.

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **One-time Setup Fee:** ~$1,000.00 per district.
- **Annual Cost:** ~$3.00 to $5.00 per student-athlete / school year.
- **Support:** Includes free training and 24/7/365 support for admins and parents.
- **Billing:** Typically directed to the district office.

## 4. Integrations
- **SIS Integration:** Syncs with PowerSchool, ProgressBook, and other major Student Information Systems.
- **Eventlink:** Partnership for unified event management, scheduling, and scores.
- **ATS EMR:** Integrates with athletic trainer software for injury and concussion management.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Scope:** Primarily focused on form compliance; does not have the deep video analysis of Hudl or the official assigning of ArbiterSports.
- **Parental Effort:** While data is reused, parents still need to log in annually to sign forms.
- **Niche Focus:** Districts may still need other tools for scheduling and game management.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rival:** ArbiterSports (FamilyID), DragonFly Max (wider feature set, but more complex).
- **Edge:** "Compliance-first" philosophy; the most trusted tool for reducing district risk and liability.
- **API Status:** Secure database connections for SIS syncing; focuses on data privacy (FERPA compliant).
