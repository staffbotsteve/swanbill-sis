# Hudl Feature Spec: Athletic Video & Performance

## 1. Overview
Hudl is a global leader in sports video analysis and performance software for high schools. It is the dominant choice for football and basketball programs that need deep film breakdown, tactical analysis, and recruiting tools.

## 2. Core Features
### Video Capture & Analysis
- **Hudl Focus:** Smart cameras (Indoor, Outdoor, Flex) that automatically capture and upload games/practices without a camera operator.
- **Hudl Assist:** Professional analysts break down your game or scout film within 24 hours, providing team/player stats and interactive reports.
- **Interactive Review:** Coaches and players can watch, tag, and comment on video from any device.
- **Performance Tracking:** Integrations with wearable technology (WIMU/Titan) for elite-level athlete monitoring.

### Recruiting & Highlights
- **Recruit Platform:** A massive database of student-athlete stats and video for college recruiters to search and find talent.
- **Highlight Reels:** Athletes can create their own highlight videos (manual or AI-generated) to share with college coaches.
- **Direct Messaging:** Athletes can send personalized messages and their Hudl profiles directly to college recruiters.
- **Elite 11 Partnership (2026):** Verified performance metrics and video for top high school quarterbacks.

### Team Management
- **Communication App:** Secure messaging for coaches, athletes, and parents.
- **Scheduling & Forms:** Tools for managing team schedules and digital forms.
- **Signal Platform:** Cloud-based athlete monitoring for injury tracking and training load.

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **Individual Sport Subscription (e.g., Basketball):**
    - Bronze: ~$400/year (100 hours storage)
    - Silver: ~$1,000/year (250 hours storage)
    - Gold: ~$1,600/year (500 hours storage)
- **Hudl Assist (Add-on):**
    - 10 games: ~$399
    - 30 games: ~$999
- **Platinum / Department Package:**
    - High-end tiers (Platinum+) start at ~$4,000/year for individual sports.
    - Department-wide packages for all sports require a custom quote.

## 4. Integrations
- **MaxPreps:** Syncs stats and scores directly to MaxPreps.com.
- **Wyscout / Instat / Statsbomb:** Access to professional-grade scouting and data libraries for various sports.
- **Eventlink:** For streamlined athletic event management and scheduling.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Cost:** Hudl is the most expensive option on the market; small schools or programs with limited budgets can find it prohibitive.
- **Learning Curve:** Advanced analysis tools like Sportscode have a steep learning curve for non-technical coaches.
- **Storage Limits:** Lower-tier plans can quickly run out of video storage hours.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rival:** MaxPreps Advantage (lower cost, deeper exposure), Sportsvisio (AI-based alternative).
- **Edge:** The "gold standard" for film study; if a high school wants a "pro-style" film room, they use Hudl.
- **API Status:** Private REST API used primarily for official partnerships (MaxPreps, state associations).
