# SCOIR Feature Specification
**Product:** SCOIR
**Category:** College and Career Readiness
**Target Audience:** K-12 Students, Parents, School Counselors, and Higher-Ed Admissions.

---

## 1. Core Features

### College Search & Discovery
*   **Modern Interface:** Visually driven, "social-media-like" experience for student engagement.
*   **College Clusters:** Groupings of similar schools to help students discover institutions outside of "name-brand" recognition.
*   **Personalized Financials:** Integrated net price calculators and financial aid estimates based on family-provided data.
*   **Side-by-Side Comparison:** Interactive tools to compare up to 4 colleges on cost, size, and outcomes.

### Student Portfolio & Career Exploration
*   **PrinciplesYou:** Collaboration with Principles (Ray Dalio) for high-fidelity personality and career matching.
*   **Student Portfolio:** Digital resume and profile builder that students can share with colleges.
*   **Activity Tracking:** Logging of extracurriculars, awards, and work experience throughout high school.

### Application Management
*   **Drag-and-Drop Workflow:** Kanban-style board for tracking colleges from "Following" to "Applying" to "Applied."
*   **Deadline Management:** Automatic alerts for Early Decision, Early Action, and Regular Decision dates.
*   **Direct Application:** Supports direct application submission for Coalition for College member schools.

### Document Delivery
*   **Cloud-Based eDocs:** Real-time tracking of transcript and recommendation letter status.
*   **Resubmission Capability:** Allows counselors to resubmit updated documents (e.g., corrected grades) after initial delivery.
*   **Common App Integration:** Streamlined "one-click" matching process to eliminate common syncing errors.

### Counselor & Parent Access
*   **Counselor Dashboard:** Real-time feed of student activity, application progress, and document requests.
*   **Parent Portal:** Engagement tools for parents to suggest colleges and view financial aid estimates.
*   **Office Hours:** Scheduling tool for counselors to manage student appointments within the platform.

---

## 2. Integration Capabilities
*   **Common App:** Native, automatic sync of student IDs and document delivery statuses.
*   **SIS Integration:** Supports Clever Secure Sync for rostering; building native integrations with major SIS providers.
*   **Slate (Technolutions):** Direct integration with Slate to facilitate seamless data flow to college admissions offices.

---

## 3. Pricing Model
*   **Structure:** Per-student annual subscription.
*   **Estimated Cost:** Reported at approximately **$4.00 - $5.00 per student** (significantly cheaper than Naviance).
*   **Free for Families:** No cost to students or parents.

---

## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### Comparison to Naviance
*   **UX:** SCOIR is widely considered modern and intuitive; Naviance is often described as dated.
*   **Document Delivery:** SCOIR allows document resubmission; Naviance historically makes this more difficult.
*   **Data Strategy:** Naviance relies on historical "Scattergrams"; SCOIR focuses more on "Admission Intelligence" and forward-looking discovery.
*   **Cost:** SCOIR is typically 30-50% less expensive than Naviance.

### Pain Points
*   **Smaller Historical Data:** Does not have the decades of school-specific admissions data that Naviance possesses.
*   **Feature Gaps:** Some advanced career-planning tools and multi-year course planning features in Naviance are not yet fully matched by SCOIR.

### Competitive Advantages
*   **Speed & Reliability:** Faster sync with Common App and more reliable document delivery.
*   **Financial Transparency:** Best-in-class tools for estimating the real cost of college for families.
