# Naviance Feature Specification
**Product:** Naviance by PowerSchool
**Category:** College, Career, and Life Readiness (CCLR)
**Target Audience:** K-12 Students, Parents, School Counselors, and Administrators.

---

## 1. Core Features

### College Search & Exploration
*   **SuperMatch:** Advanced search with 30+ attributes (size, location, major, selectivity, student life).
*   **Scattergrams:** Data visualization plotting a high school's historical GPA/test score data against college acceptance results to show "likelihood of admission."
*   **College Compare:** Side-by-side evaluation of student stats vs. average stats of accepted students from their specific school.
*   **College Visit Management:** Scheduling and tracking college representative visits to the high school.

### Career Assessments & Discovery
*   **Self-Discovery Suite:** Career Cluster Finder, Career Interest Profiler, "Do What You Are" (Personality), StrengthsExplorer, and Learning Style Inventory.
*   **Roadtrip Nation:** Archive of 3,500+ videos featuring leaders across various industries to inspire career paths.
*   **Career Profiles:** Detailed data on wages, growth projections, and required education for thousands of jobs.

### Scholarship Tracking
*   **National Database:** Powered by Sallie Mae, providing access to billions in scholarship funds.
*   **Local Scholarship Management:** Tools for counselors to upload and track scholarships specific to their district/region.
*   **Favorite List:** Students and parents can track application deadlines and award amounts.

### Course Planning
*   **Multi-Year Plans:** Students can build 4-year course plans aligned with graduation requirements and post-secondary goals.
*   **Graduation Progress Tracking:** Real-time visibility into whether a student is meeting state or district requirements.

### Document Delivery (eDocs)
*   **Electronic Submission:** Sending transcripts, school reports, and mid-year reports to over 2,000+ colleges.
*   **Teacher Recommendations:** Automated request and tracking system for letters of recommendation.
*   **Common App Integration:** Syncing of student data and documents with the Common Application.

### Parent & Counselor Tools
*   **Parent Portal:** View-only access for parents to monitor student progress, career assessments, and college lists.
*   **Counselor Dashboard:** Centralized hub for managing student records, sending bulk communications, and tracking application statuses school-wide.
*   **Reporting & Analytics:** Advanced insights into school-wide CCLR outcomes, enrollment trends, and student engagement metrics.

---

## 2. Integration Capabilities
*   **SIS Integration:** Deep, native integration with PowerSchool SIS and eSchoolPlus. Supports Clever for SSO and roster syncing.
*   **LMS Integration:** Syncs with Schoology Learning for unified curriculum access.
*   **Common App:** Two-way sync for application status and document matching.

---

## 3. Pricing Model
*   **Structure:** Custom quotation-based pricing.
*   **Estimated Cost:** Historically reported at approximately **$7.00 - $10.00 per student** annually.
*   **Implementation:** Usually involves a one-time setup fee for data migration and training.

---

## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### Pain Points
*   **Outdated UX:** Many users report the interface feels "clunky" compared to newer competitors like SCOIR.
*   **Technical Glitches:** Frequent complaints regarding the reliability of the Common App sync and document delivery status updates.
*   **Cost:** Generally higher per-student cost than emerging alternatives.

### Competitive Advantages
*   **Market Dominance:** Largest database of historical "Scattergram" data in the industry.
*   **Ecosystem:** Part of the PowerSchool ecosystem, making it the "default" choice for many large districts.
*   **Exhaustive Data:** Depth of career assessments and reporting is often superior to entry-level competitors.
