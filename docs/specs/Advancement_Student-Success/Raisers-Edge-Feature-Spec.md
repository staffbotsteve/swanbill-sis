# Raiser's Edge NXT Feature Specification
**Product:** Raiser's Edge NXT (Blackbaud)
**Category:** Fundraising & Donor Management CRM
**Target Audience:** Mid-to-Large Nonprofits, Private Schools, and Universities.

---

## 1. Core Features

### Donor Management (Constituents)
*   **360-Degree Profiles:** Centralized view of donor history, wealth data, relationships, and communication logs.
*   **Automatic Data Health:** Integrated AddressFinder and DeceasedRecordFinder to keep lists current.
*   **Segmentation:** Advanced filtering to create lists for targeted appeals (e.g., "LYBUNT" - Last Year But Unfortunately Not This year).

### Gift Processing & Management
*   **Batch Entry:** High-volume gift entry tools for checks and offline donations.
*   **Online Giving:** Customizable donation forms with automated receipting and acknowledgment letters.
*   **Recurring & Pledged Gifts:** Tracking of multi-year pledges and automated monthly giving cycles.
*   **Tributes & Soft Credits:** Managing honor/memorial gifts and credits for influencers/spouses.

### Campaigns & Appeals
*   **Campaign Hierarchy:** Organizing fundraising by Project, Fund, and Appeal.
*   **Performance Dashboards:** Real-time visualization of campaign ROI and progress toward goals.
*   **Email Marketing:** Built-in templates for solicitation and stewardship emails.

### Prospect Research & Major Giving
*   **Wealth Screening:** Integrated tools to identify "hidden" wealth within the donor base.
*   **Moves Management:** Tracking the "pipeline" of major gift prospects through Identification, Cultivation, Solicitation, and Stewardship.
*   **AI-Powered Insights:** "Likelihood to Give" scores and suggested "Ask Amounts" powered by Blackbaud Intelligence.

### Events & Volunteer Management
*   **Event Registration:** Managing ticket sales, seating charts, and participant dietary requirements.
*   **Volunteer Tracking:** Logging hours and skills for supporters who contribute time.

---

## 2. Integration Capabilities
*   **Microsoft Office 365:** Direct sync with Outlook for email logging and Excel for reporting.
*   **Blackbaud Merchant Services (BBMS):** Native payment processing.
*   **API (SKY API):** Open API for connecting to third-party marketing, accounting (e.g., Financial Edge), and SIS tools.

---

## 3. Pricing Model
*   **Structure:** Tiered subscription based on the number of records (contacts) and number of users.
*   **Estimated Cost:**
    *   **Starter:** ~$10,000 - $15,000 / year.
    *   **Mid-Market:** ~$30,000 - $60,000 / year.
    *   **Enterprise:** $100,000+ / year.
*   **Additional Costs:** Implementation fees are typically 50-100% of the first-year license fee.

---

## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### Pain Points
*   **Complexity:** Steep learning curve; often requires a dedicated "database manager."
*   **Cost:** One of the most expensive options in the nonprofit market.
*   **Customer Support:** Historically criticized for slow response times and "siloed" support structures.
*   **Contract Lock-in:** Known for aggressive multi-year contracts.

### Competitive Advantages
*   **Comprehensive:** No other tool offers the same depth of features specifically for fundraising.
*   **Reporting:** The standard for nonprofit financial reporting and board-level dashboards.
*   **AI Leadership:** Significant investment in predictive analytics for donor behavior.
