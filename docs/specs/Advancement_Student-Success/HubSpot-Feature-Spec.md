# HubSpot Feature Specification
**Product:** HubSpot for Education
**Category:** Marketing, Admissions, and Student Engagement CRM
**Target Audience:** Independent Schools, Charter Schools, and Higher-Ed Admissions.

---

## 1. Core Features

### Marketing & Inbound Recruitment
*   **Lead Capture:** High-conversion landing pages and embedded forms for inquiries.
*   **Email Marketing:** Drag-and-drop editor with A/B testing and personalization tokens.
*   **SEO & Content Tools:** Integrated blog platform with AI-powered SEO recommendations to drive organic traffic to school sites.
*   **Social Media Management:** Scheduling and monitoring student/parent engagement across platforms.

### Admissions CRM (Sales Hub)
*   **Pipeline Management:** Visual "deals" board to track students from "Inquiry" to "Applied" to "Enrolled."
*   **Meeting Scheduler:** Personalized links for admissions officers to book tours or interviews.
*   **360-Degree View:** Timeline of every interaction (email open, website visit, form submission) a prospect has had.

### Workflows & Automation
*   **Lead Nurturing:** Automated "drip" campaigns that trigger when a parent downloads a prospectus.
*   **Internal Tasks:** Automatically assign follow-up tasks to admissions staff based on prospect behavior.
*   **Data Cleaning:** Workflows to format phone numbers, capitalize names, and remove duplicates.

### Student Support & Service
*   **Ticketing System:** Managing student or parent inquiries as support tickets.
*   **Knowledge Base:** Hosting "Frequently Asked Questions" for parents and current students.

### Reporting & Analytics
*   **Attribution Reporting:** Tracking which marketing channel (Google Ads vs. Social vs. Organic) drove the most enrollments.
*   **Custom Dashboards:** Real-time visibility into enrollment funnels and staff productivity.

---

## 2. Pricing Tiers

### Free Tools
*   **Features:** Basic CRM, limited email marketing (2,000 sends/mo), forms, and live chat.
*   **Best For:** Small schools or those just starting with digital recruitment.

### Starter (~$15 - $50/mo)
*   **Features:** Removal of HubSpot branding, increased email limits, and basic automation.

### Professional (~$800 - $1,200/mo)
*   **Features:** Full automation (Workflows), custom reporting, lead scoring, and social media tools.
*   **Education Use Case:** Most K-12 independent schools land here for comprehensive admissions management.

### Enterprise (~$3,600+/mo)
*   **Features:** Advanced permissions, multi-domain support, custom objects, and predictive lead scoring.
*   **Education Use Case:** Large university systems or multi-campus school networks.

---

## 3. Integration Capabilities
*   **App Marketplace:** 1,500+ integrations (Slack, Zoom, Google Workspace).
*   **SIS/LMS:** Connects to PowerSchool, Blackbaud, and Canvas via third-party integrators (like Zapier or Piesync) or native Ops Hub sync.

---

## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### Education-Specific Use Cases
*   **Prospective Parent Nurturing:** Automating the "Why Our School?" story over a 6-month enrollment cycle.
*   **Event Management:** Using forms and automated reminders for Open Houses.

### Pain Points
*   **Generic Language:** Uses business terms like "Sales," "Deals," and "Customers" which can be jarring for educators (requires customization).
*   **Cost Escalation:** Pricing jumps significantly from "Starter" to "Professional."
*   **Not a Student Record System:** Cannot replace a school's SIS (no grading, attendance, or scheduling).

### Competitive Advantages
*   **User Interface:** Best-in-class UX; requires minimal training.
*   **Inbound Power:** Unmatched tools for attracting and converting new families.
*   **Ecosystem:** Infinite extensibility compared to "locked-down" school software.
