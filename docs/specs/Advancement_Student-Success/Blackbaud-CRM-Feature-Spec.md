# Blackbaud CRM Feature Specification
**Product:** Blackbaud CRM
**Category:** Enterprise Advancement & Constituent Relationship Management
**Target Audience:** Large Universities, Global Nonprofits, and Healthcare Systems.

---

## 1. Core Features

### Enterprise Constituent Management
*   **Global View:** Designed to handle millions of records across multiple campuses or regional offices.
*   **Corporate Hierarchies:** Advanced tracking of corporate structures, foundations, and their relationships to individual donors.
*   **Household Management:** Complex logic for managing multi-generational families and diverse household structures.

### Advanced Fundraising & Advancement
*   **Naming Opportunities:** Managing the inventory of physical naming rights (buildings, rooms, chairs) and their associated pledges.
*   **Stewardship Plans:** Highly customized, multi-step plans for high-net-worth individuals.
*   **Fund Management:** Tracking restricted vs. unrestricted funds across massive endowments.
*   **Donor Challenges:** Native support for matching gift challenges and crowdfunding logic at an enterprise scale.

### Marketing & Communications
*   **Omnichannel Marketing:** Integrated direct mail, email, and social media tracking.
*   **Constituent Preference Center:** Granular control for donors to manage how and when they are contacted across different departments.

### Analytics & Data Warehousing
*   **Integrated BI:** Built-in Business Intelligence tools for custom data modeling.
*   **Data Warehouse:** Allows for deep SQL-level querying and integration with third-party visualization tools (like Tableau or PowerBI).
*   **Benchmarking:** Comparing institutional performance against peer organizations within the Blackbaud network.

---

## 2. Differences from Raiser's Edge (RE) NXT
*   **Scale:** RE NXT is for mid-sized orgs; Blackbaud CRM is for "The Big Ten" universities and global charities.
*   **Customizability:** Blackbaud CRM is a "platform" (similar to Salesforce) that can be heavily customized; RE NXT is more "out-of-the-box."
*   **Architecture:** Blackbaud CRM offers deeper data warehouse capabilities and multi-tenant management.

---

## 3. Integration Capabilities
*   **ResearchPoint:** Native integration for deep-dive prospect research.
*   **Financial Edge NXT:** Seamless syncing with Blackbaud’s enterprise accounting suite.
*   **API Infrastructure:** Robust, enterprise-grade APIs for high-volume data synchronization.

---

## 4. Pricing Model
*   **Structure:** High-entry enterprise licensing.
*   **Estimated Cost:** Generally starts at **$150,000 - $250,000+ per year**.
*   **Implementation:** Often involves year-long implementation cycles with six-figure consulting fees.

---

## 5. Competitive Intelligence

### Pain Points
*   **Total Cost of Ownership:** Extremely high; requires internal IT/Developer resources to maintain.
*   **User Experience:** Can be overwhelming for non-technical staff due to the sheer volume of fields and options.
*   **Implementation Risk:** Long deployment cycles often lead to "scope creep" and budget overruns.

### Competitive Advantages
*   **Unrivaled Scale:** The only platform truly built for the complexity of massive higher-ed advancement offices.
*   **All-in-One Identity:** Eliminates the "data silos" found in organizations using multiple disparate systems.
*   **Wealth Data:** Deepest integration with proprietary Blackbaud wealth and philanthropic data.
