# Remind Feature Spec — Teacher/Parent Messaging

## Overview
Remind is a popular messaging platform that connects teachers, students, and parents in a secure, privacy-first environment. It is designed to make school communication as easy as text messaging without revealing personal phone numbers.

## Core Features

### 1. Secure Messaging (Remind Chat)
*   **Two-Way Communication:** Secure messaging for individuals, small groups, or entire classes.
*   **Privacy-First:** Personal phone numbers are never shared; communication occurs within the app or via a Remind-specific number.
*   **Multimedia Support:** Send photos, voice clips, files, and links directly to families.
*   **Read Receipts:** Teachers can track who has received and opened their messages.
*   **Quick Reply Stamps:** Pre-set emojis/stamps to speed up responses.

### 2. School & District Announcements (Remind Hub)
*   **Mass Notifications:** Send announcements across a school or district via text, app, or email.
*   **Urgent Messaging:** Critical alerts (e.g., closures) with text-to-voice fallback for unreachable users.
*   **Scheduling:** Compose announcements in advance and schedule for a specific date and time.
*   **Targeted Messaging:** Filter announcements by role (student, parent, teacher) or SIS-synced data (grade level, course).

### 3. Translation & Equity
*   **Two-Way Translation:** Automatic translation into over 90 languages (powered by Google Translate).
*   **SIS Language Sync:** Automatically sets a parent's preferred language based on data from the SIS.
*   **Edit Translations:** Teachers can edit auto-translations on the web to ensure accuracy.

### 4. Administrative Oversight
*   **Message History:** Admins can request and download message transcripts for compliance/security.
*   **Rostering:** Auto-provisioning of accounts via SIS integration.
*   **Engagement Stats:** Track message volume and reach across the entire organization.
*   **Voice Calls:** Paid plans (Remind Hub) allow for secure voice calls within the platform.

## Integrations
*   **SIS:** PowerSchool, Aeries, Infinite Campus, Skyward, and Blackbaud.
*   **LMS:** Google Classroom, Canvas, and Blackboard.
*   **General:** Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Box, and Dropbox.

## Pricing
*   **Free (Remind Chat):** Up to 10 classes and 150 participants per class. Basic messaging and file sharing.
*   **Paid (Remind Hub/Premium):** Custom pricing based on enrollment. Includes urgent messaging, voice calls, SIS rostering, and higher participant limits (up to 5,000/class).
*   **Positioning:** Remind is the "messaging-first" choice for schools that want high adoption through the simplicity of text messaging.

## Pain Points
*   **Message Length:** 300-character limit on the Free plan (similar to SMS).
*   **Uneditable Messages:** Sent messages cannot be edited or deleted, which can lead to errors.
*   **Pricing Complexity:** Moving from the free tier to a school-wide paid plan can be a significant cost jump.

## Competitive Intelligence

### Remind vs. ParentSquare
| Feature | Remind | ParentSquare |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **User Experience** | Feels like Texting | Feels like a School Portal |
| **Ease of Use** | Extremely High (Instant) | Moderate (Higher Learning) |
| **Messaging Focus** | Primarily Two-Way | Multimodal Engagement |
| **Forms/Slips** | No (File sharing only) | Yes (Built-in slips/forms) |
| **Urgent Alerts** | Yes (Hub Plan) | Yes (Built-in) |

*   **Key Advantage:** Remind is the leader in "engagement through simplicity." Because it feels and acts like text messaging, it often achieves higher adoption rates among families who may not want to manage a separate school "app."
