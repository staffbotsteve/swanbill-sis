# FACTS Tuition Management Feature Spec

## Overview
FACTS Tuition Management is a leading cloud-based financial management solution specifically designed for K-12 private and faith-based schools. It is a division of Nelnet Inc. and is part of a broader ecosystem that includes SIS, admissions, and financial aid tools.

## Core Features
### Tuition Billing & Payment Plans
*   **Automated Billing:** Centralized collection of tuition and fees with scheduled automated invoicing.
*   **Customizable Payment Plans:** Schools can offer monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or custom installments to spread the cost.
*   **Incidental Billing:** Ability to bill for non-tuition items (field trips, books, sports fees) separately or as part of the main invoice.
*   **Prepay Accounts:** Support for cafeteria or after-school care funds where parents load money in advance.

### Payment Processing
*   **Methods:** Supports ACH (automatic bank withdrawal), Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Amex), and Debit Cards.
*   **Auto-Pay:** Families can sign up for automatic deductions to ensure on-time payments.
*   **Mobile Access:** Payments can be made via mobile-responsive portals.

### Parent Portal
*   **Real-time Access:** Parents can view account balances, payment history, and upcoming due dates 24/7.
*   **Communication:** Automated payment reminders via email and text to reduce late payments.
*   **Self-Service:** Ability for parents to update payment methods and contact information.

### Financial Aid & Grant Management
*   **FACTS Grant & Aid Assessment:** (Integrated module) Comprehensive financial aid application process.
*   **IRS Integration:** Direct connection to the IRS to pull tax transcripts (W-2s, 1040s), reducing manual document uploads and fraud risk.
*   **Award Application:** Financial aid awards can be automatically applied to tuition accounts once approved.

### Reporting & Analytics
*   **Real-time Financials:** Dashboards for administrators to track aging, delinquency, and total collections.
*   **Cash Flow Forecasting:** Tools to predict future revenue based on scheduled payment plans.
*   **Audit Trails:** Secure, encrypted transaction history for all financial activities.

## Integration Capabilities
*   **FACTS Ecosystem:** Native integration with FACTS SIS (formerly RenWeb), FACTS Application & Enrollment, and FACTS Giving.
*   **Third-Party SIS:** Integrates with various other SIS platforms via API or scheduled exports (e.g., Blackbaud, PowerSchool).

## Pricing (Estimated)
*   **Enrollment Fees (Family Paid):**
    *   $25 per family for 1-2 payments per year.
    *   $55 per family for 3+ payments (recurring plans).
*   **Processing Fees:** ~2.85% for credit card transactions.
*   **Returned Payment Fee:** $30 per occurrence.
*   **Peace of Mind Benefit:** $22.50 (optional insurance covering tuition in case of parent death).

## Pain Points
*   **Fragmented Pricing:** Many small fees can add up and be confusing for families.
*   **System Complexity:** Some users find certain administrative workflows cumbersome or \"click-heavy.\"
*   **Support Speed:** Being part of a large corporation (Nelnet), some schools report slower support response times during peak seasons.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **vs. Blackbaud (Smart Tuition):** FACTS is often perceived as more affordable and easier to adopt for smaller to mid-sized schools, whereas Blackbaud is seen as the \"enterprise\" choice for large, complex institutions.
*   **Market Position:** Market leader in the Catholic and Christian school segments.
*   **Key Strength:** The IRS integration for financial aid is a significant differentiator that saves hundreds of hours in manual verification.
