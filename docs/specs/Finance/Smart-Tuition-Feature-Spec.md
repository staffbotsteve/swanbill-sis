# Smart Tuition (Blackbaud Tuition Management) Feature Spec

## Overview
Smart Tuition was acquired by Blackbaud and rebranded as **Blackbaud Tuition Management**. It is a high-end financial solution designed for K-12 private schools that require deep integration with the Blackbaud ecosystem and professional-grade delinquency management.

## Core Features
### Tuition & Fee Management
*   **Family-Centric Billing:** Consolidates all students in a household into a single billing statement.
*   **Flexible Payment Methods:** Supports ACH, Credit/Debit cards, and "Pay Near Me" (cash payments at retail locations).
*   **Mobile App:** Dedicated parent app for managing payments and viewing statements on the go.

### Delinquency & Follow-up
*   **Professional Follow-up:** Blackbaud provides dedicated staff to follow up on late payments, acting as an extension of the school's business office.
*   **Automated Late Fees:** Configurable rules for late fee application and grace periods.

### Payment Processing
*   **PCI Compliance:** Level 1 PCI-DSS compliant with 256-bit encryption.
*   **Scheduled Payments:** Strong focus on automated recurring billing to ensure predictable cash flow.

### Financial Aid Integration
*   **Blackbaud Financial Aid Management:** Seamlessly connects financial aid applications to the billing system, ensuring awards are accurately reflected on invoices.

### Reporting
*   **Financial Edge NXT Integration:** Deep integration with Blackbaud's accounting software for one-click reconciliation.
*   **Delinquency Tracking:** Detailed reports on aging and payment trends across the entire school population.

## Differences from FACTS Tuition Management
*   **Ecosystem:** Smart Tuition is part of the Blackbaud "Total School" suite (Financial Edge, Raiser's Edge, Education Management). FACTS is part of the Nelnet/FACTS ecosystem.
*   **Collection Philosophy:** Blackbaud places a heavier emphasis on their "professional follow-up" service to reduce administrative burden on the school's business office.
*   **Pricing Structure:** Generally considered more expensive than FACTS, often requiring additional modules for full functionality.

## Integration Capabilities
*   **Native:** Blackbaud SIS, Financial Edge NXT (Accounting), Raiser's Edge NXT (Fundraising).
*   **API:** Robust API for custom integrations with non-Blackbaud systems.

## Pricing (Estimated)
*   **Annual Fees:** Custom quotes based on school size.
*   **Transaction Fees:** Competitive with industry standards (standard CC processing rates).
*   **Note:** Often bundled into larger Blackbaud enterprise contracts.

## Pain Points
*   **High Cost:** Implementation and subscription costs can be prohibitive for smaller schools.
*   **Complexity:** The depth of features can lead to a steep learning curve for new administrators.
*   **Sales Pressure:** Users often report pressure to purchase additional modules within the Blackbaud suite.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **vs. FACTS:** Blackbaud is the "gold standard" for large, well-endowed independent schools that already use Blackbaud for SIS and Fundraising.
*   **Key Strength:** The level of professional service provided (follow-up calls to parents) is a major selling point for schools with small business offices.
