# Feature Spec: Acceptiva (Donation & Non-Profit Focus)

## 1. Overview
Acceptiva is a specialized donation processing platform for the non-profit and education sectors. Now part of the Qgiv family, it focuses on high-security, low-cost merchant accounts with school-specific branding.

## 2. Feature List
### Donation Processing
- **Custom Branded Forms:** Online and mobile-responsive forms that match the school's identity.
- **Recurring Gifts:** Automatic weekly, monthly, or quarterly donations for sustainable giving.
- **Gift Matching:** Integration tools for employers to match parent or alumni contributions.
- **Event Ticketing:** Built-in tools for fundraising galas, auctions, and alumni dinners.
- **Secure Processing:** Level 1 PCI DSS certification for peace of mind.

### Fundraising Tools (via Qgiv)
- **Peer-to-Peer Fundraising:** Empowering parents or alumni to fundraise on behalf of the school.
- **Text Giving:** Quick "text-to-donate" functionality for live school events.
- **Auction Management:** Full-featured bidding and checkout for silent or live auctions.
- **Attendance Tracking:** Check-in tools for event attendees.

### Reporting & Reconciliation
- **24/7 Portal Access:** Secure portal for tracking donations and settlement reports.
- **Automated Receipts:** Custom email receipts sent immediately after every transaction.
- **Real-Time Data:** Gain actionable donor insights for targeted future campaigns.

## 3. Pricing (Education/Nonprofit)
| Service | Estimate |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Monthly Subscription** | Starts around $99/mo |
| **Qgiv Base Plan** | $0 + transaction fees (for smaller orgs) |
| **Add-on Packages** | $40–$259/mo (P2P, Text, Auctions) |

*Note: Pricing is typically customized based on the school's volume and needed features.*

## 4. Integration Patterns
- **Merchant Account Integration:** Specialized accounts designed for non-profit fee structures.
- **CRM Sync:** Data flow into major non-profit CRMs (Salesforce, Raiser's Edge).
- **RESTful API:** Available for custom school portal connections.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Consolidated Brand:** Some confusion exists since the Qgiv acquisition (two different websites/brands).
- **Interface Complexity:** The abundance of features (P2P, auctions, text) can be overwhelming for smaller schools.
- **Customization Limits:** Branded forms have limits compared to a fully custom-coded Stripe solution.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Acceptiva is for Donors:** More focused on the "gift" experience than a standard tuition payment gateway.
- **Better than PayPal for Non-profits:** More sophisticated recurring giving and donor tracking than a simple "Donate" button.
- **The Winner:** Best for private schools or foundations with heavy fundraising and alumni engagement requirements.
