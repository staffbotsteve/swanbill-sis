# TADS Feature Spec

## Overview
TADS (Tuition and Admission Data Services) is a comprehensive suite of school management tools owned by CommunityBrands. It is known for its "hands-on" service model and focus on the entire lifecycle of a student, from application to graduation.

## Core Features
### Tuition Management
*   **Line-Item Billing:** Extremely detailed billing that allows schools to track every charge and credit individually.
*   **Agreement Builder:** Patent-pending tool to create, brand, and track signed digital enrollment contracts.
*   **Delinquency Management:** Automated calls and emails for payment reminders, with a focus on maintain family relationships.

### Financial Aid Assessment
*   **Formula-Driven:** Uses a vetted formula with 140+ data points to determine "true" financial need, rather than just simple income brackets.
*   **Audit-Ready:** Provides a transparent trail of how every aid decision was reached.
*   **Multilingual Support:** Applications and support available in English and Spanish.

### Admissions & Enrollment
*   **Integrated Workflow:** Data flows seamlessly from the initial application to the enrollment contract and then into the tuition system.
*   **Document Management:** Families can upload transcripts, health forms, and recommendations directly to the portal.
*   **Real-time Tracking:** Administrators can see exactly where every family is in the funnel.

### Payment Processing
*   **TADS Flex Model:** A modern pricing model where schools pay a flat per-student rate and have total control over fee waivers.
*   **Payment Methods:** ACH, Credit/Debit, paper checks.

## Pricing (Estimated)
*   **TADS Flex:** Flat per-student subscription fee (contact for quote).
*   **Traditional Model (Family Paid Fees):**
    *   **Agreement Fee:** $40 (1-2 payments) to $55 (10 payments) per year.
    *   **Credit Card Fee:** ~3%.
    *   **ACH Fee:** Often tiered; payments < $200 may be free, while higher amounts incur a fee.
*   **Late Fees:** Typically 1% of balance or a flat fee (e.g., $50) depending on school policy.

## Integration Capabilities
*   **Educate SIS:** Native integration with TADS' own SIS.
*   **Partner Integrations:** Works with various 3rd party SIS and accounting packages via export/import.
*   **IRS Tax Data:** Partnership with SSS to authorize direct IRS data retrieval.

## Pain Points
*   **Legacy UI:** Some parts of the administrative interface feel dated compared to newer competitors.
*   **Fee Transparency:** Families sometimes complain about the variety of administrative and convenience fees.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **vs. FACTS/Blackbaud:** TADS is often praised for having superior customer support for families, acting as a true "customer service" arm for the school.
*   **Key Strength:** The Agreement Builder is a standout feature that streamlines the legal side of enrollment much better than many generic form builders.
