# Feature Spec: Permission Click (Digital Forms & Payments)

## 1. Overview
Permission Click is a specialized K-12 platform for automating the intersection of permission slips and payments. It solves the "paper trail" problem for field trips, athletic waivers, and start-of-year forms.

## 2. Feature List
### Digital Permission Slips
- **Drag-and-Drop Builder:** Easy creation of complex forms with conditional logic.
- **Electronic Signatures:** Legal, audit-ready signatures from parents on any device.
- **Multilingual Support:** Auto-translation into 100+ languages for diverse school communities.
- **Parent Portal:** No account required for parents to sign and pay, reducing friction.

### School Payment Collection (SafePay)
- **Activity Fees:** Combined signing and paying for field trips or retreats in one flow.
- **Digital Wallets:** Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Microsoft Pay support.
- **Reporting:** Automatic deposit and financial reports for school bookkeepers.
- **Partial Refunds:** Easy handling of canceled trips or individual student changes.

### Reporting & Analytics
- **Form Dashboard:** Real-time visibility into who has signed/paid.
- **SIS Extract Schedule:** Automated data exports to keep the SIS up to date.
- **Response Management:** Filters by student, class, or grade level.

## 3. Pricing
| Segment | Pricing Model |
| :--- | :--- |
| **School Essentials** | $1 per student / year (approx.) |
| **District Edition** | Custom (often makes Essentials free for schools) |
| **SafePay Fees** | No setup/license fees; standard transaction % only |

*Note: Often requires a 3-year contract for discounted per-student rates.*

## 4. Integration Patterns
- **SIS Sync:** Direct integrations with PowerSchool, Blackbaud, and Infinite Campus.
- **Pre-Population:** Forms can be pre-filled with student data from the SIS to save parents time.
- **Microsoft AD:** Role-based access for school staff and administrators.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Niche Focus:** While excellent for forms, it is not a full SIS or a full e-commerce store.
- **Contract Commitment:** Often requires multi-year agreements.
- **External Dependency:** If the SIS sync breaks, manual data reconciliation is labor-intensive.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Permission Click vs. Paper:** 10x faster response time from parents.
- **Permission Click vs. General Forms (Google/Typeform):** Built-in payment security and SIS sync make it the safer, more professional choice for K-12.
- **The Winner:** Best for districts looking to digitize the "paper pile" and collect activity fees securely without a full-blown SIS overhaul.
