# Feature Spec: TicketSpice (School Event Ticketing)

## 1. Overview
TicketSpice is a powerful white-label ticketing platform for school events. It is a favorite for theaters, athletics, and galas because of its affordable pricing and deep feature set.

## 2. Feature List
### Event Ticketing
- **Drag-and-Drop Builder:** Custom pages that match the school's brand for a professional feel.
- **Timed Entry:** Essential for school tours, art shows, or graduation ceremonies.
- **QR Code Scanning:** High-speed scanning for quick entry into sports games or theater performances.
- **Box Office App:** Mobile tools for selling tickets and processing payments on-site.
- **Add-ons & Upgrades:** Sell spirit wear, parking passes, or "front row" upgrades alongside tickets.

### Seating Charts
- **Interactive Map:** Drag-and-drop builder for theaters, auditoriums, and arenas.
- **Unlimited Tiers:** Different pricing for students, parents, and VIP alumni.
- **Seat View Preview:** Let parents see the view from their seats before they buy.
- **Pool Seat Holds:** Reserve blocks of seats for families or staff.

### Customization & White-Labeling
- **Full Branding Control:** No "TicketSpice" logos on your pages; parents stay on your domain.
- **Conditional Logic:** Show or hide ticket types based on the buyer's answers.
- **Custom Checkout:** Only collect the data the school needs.

## 3. Pricing
| Service | Rate |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Ticketing Fee** | $0.99 per ticket (paid events) |
| **Credit Card Fee** | 2.9% + $0.30 (standard) |
| **Free Events** | $0.99 per ticket fee still applies |

*Note: All fees can be passed on to the buyer, making the software free for the school.*

## 4. Integration Patterns
- **Zapier:** Connect to thousands of apps (Mailchimp, Salesforce, HubSpot).
- **Webhooks:** Instant updates for attendee lists or ticket sales.
- **Reporting & Export:** Bulk CSV/Excel exports for school event coordination.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Free Event Cost:** Unlike some competitors, TicketSpice still charges $0.99 for free tickets.
- **External Data:** Attendee data is separate from the main student SIS until manual export.
- **Learning Curve:** The powerful seating chart builder can be complex for a first-time user.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **TicketSpice vs. Eventbrite:** TicketSpice is much cheaper and offers more branding control.
- **White-Label Advantage:** Best for schools that want a premium, high-end "In-House" ticketing experience.
- **The Winner:** Best for private school theaters and high-school athletic departments that need professional seating charts.
