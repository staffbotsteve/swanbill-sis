# Sage Intacct for Education Feature Spec

## Overview
Sage Intacct is a best-in-class cloud financial management system. For the education sector, it is the premier choice for large private schools, multi-campus districts, and higher education institutions that require "true" fund accounting and complex dimensional reporting.

## Core Features
### Dimensional General Ledger
*   **The "Dimensions" Engine:** Instead of a massive, rigid chart of accounts, Intacct uses "tags" (dimensions) like Fund, Department, Location, Project, and Grant.
*   **Granular Tracking:** Allows a single transaction to be tagged with multiple attributes, enabling instant reporting on "How much did the 5th Grade Science Program spend from Grant X at Campus Y?"

### Fund Accounting
*   **Restricted Funds:** Native support for tracking restricted, temporarily restricted, and unrestricted net assets.
*   **Automated Inter-entity:** Automatically handles "Due To/Due From" entries for schools with multiple legal entities or campuses.

### Grant & Project Tracking
*   **Grant Lifecycle:** Tracks revenue and expenses against specific grants to ensure compliance and easy auditing.
*   **Project Accounting:** Useful for capital campaigns or construction projects.

### AP Automation & Budgeting
*   **Spend Management:** Built-in approval workflows to ensure spending stays within budget.
*   **Budgeting & Planning:** (Integrated module) Advanced "what-if" scenario modeling for future school years.

## Pricing (Estimated)
*   **Model:** Quote-based annual subscription.
*   **Starting Range:** ~$10,000 - $15,000 per year for a basic "Core Financials" setup.
*   **Mid-to-Large Schools:** Often $25,000 - $60,000+ per year depending on modules and user count.
*   **Implementation:** Usually 1x to 1.75x the first-year subscription cost ($10k - $30k+).

## Integration Capabilities
*   **Open API:** One of the most robust APIs in the accounting world.
*   **Education Ecosystem:** Integrates with high-end SIS platforms (e.g., Veracross, Blackbaud) and payroll providers (ADP, Paychex).
*   **Marketplace:** Hundreds of pre-built "Connectors" for expenses (Concur, Bill.com) and CRM (Salesforce).

## Pain Points
*   **Implementation Heavy:** Requires a professional implementation partner; this is not "plug and play."
*   **High Complexity:** Requires a skilled finance team to operate; overkill for a small school with a part-time bookkeeper.
*   **Total Cost of Ownership:** The most expensive option in this batch.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **vs. QuickBooks:** Sage Intacct is the logical "next step" for schools that find QuickBooks reporting too limited or their multi-entity management too manual.
*   **Key Strength:** The **Dimensional Reporting** is unparalleled. It allows school boards to see financial health from any angle (by campus, by fund, by program) without exporting data to Excel.
