# Google Workspace for Education Feature Spec

## 1. Overview
Google Workspace for Education is the leading productivity and collaboration suite for schools, centered around **Google Classroom** and **Drive**. It offers a tiered model: **Education Fundamentals** (Free), **Education Standard** (Paid), **Teaching and Learning Upgrade** (Paid Add-on), and **Education Plus** (Paid, comprehensive).

## 2. Core Features
### Collaborative Suite (Fundamentals)
- **Classroom:** Central hub for assignments, grading, and student communication.
- **Drive & Docs:** Real-time collaboration on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations.
- **Gmail & Meet:** Integrated communication; Meet supports up to 100 participants in the free version.
- **Gemini for Education:** AI-powered assistance with enterprise-grade data protection.

### Security & Compliance (Standard)
- **Security Center:** Prevents threats and automates monitoring (phishing, spam).
- **Security Investigation Tool:** Remediates issues across Gmail and Drive.
- **Advanced MDM:** Management of Android, iOS, and Windows devices.
- **Vault:** Data retention and eDiscovery for compliance/legal needs.

### Teaching & Learning (Upgrade Add-on)
- **Enhanced Meet:** Up to 250 participants, breakout rooms, attendance tracking, and noise cancellation.
- **Originality Reports:** Unlimited plagiarism checks in Google Classroom.
- **Storage:** Adds 100GB of pooled cloud storage per license.

### Enterprise Scale (Education Plus)
- **Meet Scale:** Up to 500 participants and 100,000 in-domain livestream viewers.
- **Roster Sync:** Automatic class creation and roster updates from SIS.
- **Personalized Cloud Search:** Intelligent search across the institution's data.
- **BigQuery Exports:** Advanced log analysis for Gmail and Classroom.

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **Education Fundamentals:** Free for qualifying institutions.
- **Education Standard:** ~$3 per student / year.
- **Teaching and Learning Upgrade:** ~$4 per license / month (~$48/year).
- **Education Plus:** ~$6 per student / year. (Includes all features and premium support).
- **Storage:** 100TB pooled across the organization in all versions; paid versions add more.

## 4. Integrations
- **SIS Integration:** Syncs via Google School Directory Sync (GSDS) or 3rd party tools like Clever/ClassLink.
- **3rd-Party Apps:** Thousands of apps in the Google Workspace Marketplace.
- **Learning Management Systems (LMS):** Deep integration with Canvas, Moodle, and Blackboard via LTI.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Storage Caps:** Transition from "unlimited" to 100TB pooled storage has forced many schools to prune data.
- **Admin Complexity:** The Admin Console is powerful but can be overwhelming for small IT teams.
- **Limited Offline Work:** While Google Docs has an offline mode, it is not as robust as Microsoft's desktop apps.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rivals:** Microsoft 365 Education (better desktop apps), Apple (stronger MDM/hardware).
- **Edge:** Dominance in K-12 due to Chromebooks and the ease of Google Classroom.
- **API Status:** Robust REST APIs for Directory, Classroom, and Drive; supports complex automation and custom app development.
