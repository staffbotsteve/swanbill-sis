# Microsoft 365 Education Feature Spec

## 1. Overview
Microsoft 365 Education is a unified productivity, collaboration, and security ecosystem. It is the dominant choice for Higher Education and many larger K-12 districts due to its robust **Teams** platform and desktop Office applications. It follows a tiered model: **A1** (Free), **A3** (Standard), and **A5** (Premium).

## 2. Core Features
### Productivity & Collaboration (A1)
- **Teams for Education:** Digital hub for classrooms, chat, assignments, and Class Notebooks.
- **Office for the Web:** Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneNote in a browser.
- **OneDrive:** Cloud storage (100GB limit per user in the free A1 version).
- **SharePoint Online:** Content and document management for staff/departments.

### Standard Desktop & Security (A3)
- **Desktop Office Apps:** Local installs of Word, Excel, PPT, Outlook, Publisher, and Access.
- **Intune for Education:** Simplified MDM for managing Windows and iOS devices.
- **Windows 11 Education:** Enterprise-grade OS for school devices.
- **Minecraft Education:** Gamified learning across various subjects.

### Advanced Security & Analytics (A5)
- **Defender for Office 365:** Protection against phishing and malware.
- **Power BI Pro:** Advanced data visualization and analytics for the whole institution.
- **Advanced Compliance:** Data Loss Prevention (DLP) and eDiscovery for sensitive information.
- **Teams Scale:** Up to 1,000 participants and 10,000 livestream viewers.
- **Audio Conferencing:** Dial-in numbers for Teams meetings.

## 3. Pricing (2026 Estimates)
- **Microsoft 365 A1:** Free for eligible institutions (Web apps only).
- **A1 for Devices:** ~$38 one-time cost per device (up to 6 years). Includes desktop apps and Intune.
- **Microsoft 365 A3:** ~$2.50 to $3.25 per user / month. (Standard for 1:1 Windows/iPad environments).
- **Microsoft 365 A5:** ~$8.00 per user / month. (Complete security and analytics package).

## 4. Integrations
- **School Data Sync (SDS):** Free service to sync rosters from SIS into Teams/A3/A5 automatically.
- **Microsoft Graph API:** Comprehensive API for schools, students, classes, and assignments.
- **3rd-Party Apps:** Integrates with Canvas, Moodle, Clever, and thousands of other edtech tools.

## 5. Pain Points
- **Complexity:** Higher learning curve for admins compared to Google Workspace.
- **Hardware Agnostic:** While Intune works on iPads, the "best" experience is often tied to Windows hardware.
- **License Management:** Managing tiered licenses across different user groups (students vs. staff) can be tedious.

## 6. Competitive Intelligence
- **Primary Rivals:** Google Workspace for Education (simpler, Chromebook-focused), Apple.
- **Edge:** The "gold standard" for staff productivity and professional preparation (students learn industry-standard tools).
- **API Status:** Microsoft Graph is the most powerful and unified API in the edtech space, allowing for deep SIS-to-LMS-to-Cloud-Office automation.
