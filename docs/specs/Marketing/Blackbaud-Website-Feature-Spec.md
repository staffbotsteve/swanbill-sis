# Blackbaud Website Feature Spec — School Website & Communications

## Overview
Blackbaud School Website CMS (part of the Blackbaud Education Management suite) is an enterprise CMS specifically optimized for schools already using the Blackbaud ecosystem. It is designed to be a unified front-end for students, parents, and alumni, deeply integrated with student records.

## Core Features

### 1. Website CMS & Design
*   **Widget-Based Builder:** Over 50 drag-and-drop widgets (photo carousels, directories, navigation, forms).
*   **Design Templates:** Provides both pre-built templates and full access to a style editor for in-house design changes.
*   **Multi-Site Management:** Ability to manage multiple sites (e.g., summer camp mini-sites, auxiliary programs) without additional hosting fees.
*   **Specialty Pages:** Academic curriculum guides and athletic team pages that pull data from the Blackbaud SIS.

### 2. Email & Communications
*   **Platform Notifications:** Basic transactional email for account management, forgotten passwords, and website invitations.
*   **E-Newsletters:** Aggregates news stories from the CMS into email templates.
*   **Emergency Alerts:** Built-in emergency notice pop-ups for the website.
*   **Limitations:** Does *not* include mass email for solicitations or fundraising (requires separate tools like Online Express or NetCommunity).

### 3. Directories & Forms
*   **Built-in Directories:** Searchable directories for staff, faculty, alumni, and admissions, with configurable privacy settings.
*   **Electronic Forms:** Supports admissions inquiry forms, secure giving forms, and medical/non-medical school forms.
*   **Form Management:** Controls for who can fill out forms and who receives notifications.

### 4. E-Commerce
*   **Integrated Payments:** Market merchandise, sell event tickets, and receive donations via Blackbaud Merchant Services.
*   **Shopping Cart:** Includes support for state taxes and secure payment processing.

## Integrations
*   **Native Ecosystem:** Seamless data flow with Blackbaud SIS, LMS, Enrollment Management, and Tuition Management.
*   **Blackbaud Marketplace & SKY API:** Extensive connectivity with Google, Canvas, Microsoft, and third-party partners via open-source APIs.
*   **External CMS:** Can also integrate with Finalsite or WordPress via the SKY API if the school prefers a different front-end.

## Pricing
*   **Model:** Custom-quoted, typically bundled as part of a larger Blackbaud Education Management subscription.
*   **Entry Points:** Often requires a substantial commitment to the Blackbaud platform (SIS/LMS/EMS).

## Pain Points
*   **Complexity:** Can be difficult to master without dedicated IT or Blackbaud-specific admins.
*   **Rigidity:** While customizable, some users find the widget-based system less flexible than modern web builders (like Webflow or Finalsite's Composer).
*   **Feature Fragmentation:** Mass email and advanced marketing require separate, paid modules.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **Key Advantage:** Deepest possible integration for schools on Blackbaud's SIS—data (like rosters, grades, schedules) flows onto the website without manual entry.
*   **Direct Competitors:** Finalsite (stronger marketing/CMS), Edlio (lower cost/simpler).
