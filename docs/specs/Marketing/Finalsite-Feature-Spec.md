# Finalsite Feature Spec — School Website CMS & Marketing

## Overview
Finalsite is a premium, enterprise-level digital experience platform (DXP) specifically designed for K-12 and higher education institutions. It combines a powerful website CMS (Composer) with enrollment management tools and strategic marketing services.

## Core Features

### 1. Website Builder & CMS (Composer)
*   **Drag-and-Drop Interface:** No-code environment for building complex, professional layouts.
*   **COPE (Create Once, Publish Everywhere):** Centralized data management where a news item or event updated in one place reflects across the website and mobile app.
*   **Module-Based Architecture:** Specialized modules for Athletics (schedules, rosters), Calendars, News, Blogs, and Directories.
*   **Personalization:** Ability to show different content to different personas (e.g., prospective families vs. current parents).

### 2. Mobile & Communications
*   **Branded Mobile App:** Fully integrated with the CMS to deliver news, alerts, and athletics.
*   **Messages XR:** Multichannel notification system supporting Email, SMS, Voice, and Push notifications from a single interface.
*   **Workflows:** Marketing automation for drip campaigns (e.g., nurturing inquiries through the enrollment funnel).

### 3. SEO & Enrollment Marketing
*   **SEO Tools:** Granular control over meta tags, alt text, and custom URLs; built-in accessibility checker (WCAG 2.1 compliance).
*   **Finalsite Enrollment (EMS):** Full student lifecycle management from inquiry to enrollment (formerly SchoolAdmin).
*   **Lead Generation:** Integrated forms and landing pages that feed directly into the enrollment CRM.

### 4. Hosting & Security
*   **Cloud Hosting:** High-availability hosting with managed security and CDN integration.
*   **SSO Integrations:** Supports SAML, Google, Azure, and major SIS logins.

## Integrations
*   **SIS/MIS:** Blackbaud, PowerSchool, FACTS, Veracross, Skyward, and Infinite Campus.
*   **LMS:** Canvas, Google Classroom, and Moodle.
*   **Payment:** Integration with Finalsite Enrollment for tuition and billing.

## Pricing
*   **Model:** Custom-quoted based on enrollment size and selected modules.
*   **Setup/Redesign:** Typically $35,000 – $75,000+ for initial custom implementation.
*   **Annual Subscription:** Usually starts at $10,000 – $20,000+ for basic CMS, scaling significantly with add-ons like Enrollment or Messages XR.

## Pain Points
*   **High Cost:** Significantly more expensive than general-purpose CMS like WordPress or Wix.
*   **Steep Learning Curve:** The depth of modules can be overwhelming for small, non-technical teams.
*   **Long Implementation:** Custom redesigns can take 6-12 months.

## Competitive Intelligence
*   **Key Advantage:** "All-in-one" platform that links the website directly to the enrollment pipeline, unlike generic tools.
*   **Direct Competitors:** Blackbaud (stronger SIS tie-ins), Edlio (simpler/cheaper), WordPress (requires heavy plugins to match Finalsite features).
