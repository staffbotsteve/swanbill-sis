# Mailchimp Feature Spec — Email Marketing & Automation

## Overview
Mailchimp (by Intuit) is a comprehensive, all-in-one marketing platform for email, automation, and audience management. It is known for its advanced data-driven features, predictive analytics, and multichannel marketing tools.

## Core Features

### 1. Email Builder & Multichannel
*   **Intuitive Editor:** Drag-and-drop builder with over 274 modern templates and custom HTML support.
*   **AI Integration:** AI-powered content generation (ChatGPT integration), predictive analytics (identifying high-value/at-risk customers), and send-time optimization.
*   **SMS Marketing:** Integrated SMS campaigns, automated triggers, and instant opt-in via popups (additional cost).
*   **WhatsApp Campaigns:** New feature for direct customer engagement.

### 2. Customer Journey Builder (Automation)
*   **Automation Journeys:** Highly customizable workflows for welcome emails, re-engagement, and abandoned cart flows.
*   **Advanced Branching:** Complex conditions (if/else), delays, and goal definitions.
*   **Single-Step Triggers:** Basic automations (Essentials plan).
*   **Multi-Step Journeys:** Advanced, pre-built journeys with branching (Standard/Premium plans).

### 3. Audience Management & Segmentation
*   **Predictive Segmentation:** AI-driven grouping based on predicted customer behavior.
*   **Dynamic Content:** Personalize email sections based on recipient data.
*   **Audience Dashboard:** Centralized view of growth, engagement, and campaign performance.
*   **A/B & Multivariate Testing:** Optimize subject lines, images, and content (Multivariate in Premium).

### 4. Reporting & Analytics
*   **Comprehensive Reports:** Click maps, revenue tracking, conversion tracking, and Google Analytics integration.
*   **Comparative Reports:** Compare campaign performance over time (Premium plan).
*   **Apple Mail Privacy:** Options to exclude automated open data for accuracy.

## Integrations
*   **E-Commerce:** Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Etsy.
*   **Business Tools:** Zapier, Salesforce, Canva, and QuickBooks (Intuit ecosystem).
*   **Marketing:** Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and social platforms.

## Pricing (2024–2026)
*   **Free Plan:** Drastically reduced (250 contacts/500 sends/month; no automation/scheduling).
*   **Essentials:** Starts at ~$13/month (500 contacts). A/B testing and basic automation.
*   **Standard:** Starts at ~$20/month (500 contacts). Multi-step journeys and predictive segmentation.
*   **Premium:** Starts at ~$350/month (10,000 contacts). Multivariate testing and priority phone support.
*   **Nonprofit Discount:** 15% discount for paid marketing plans.

## Pain Points
*   **Aggressive Pricing:** Frequent price increases and reduced limits on the Free plan.
*   **Cost of Unsubscribed Contacts:** Mailchimp charges for all contacts (including unsubscribed/inactive), making audience maintenance critical.
*   **Feature Gating:** Many "essential" features (like scheduling) now require a paid plan.

## Competitive Intelligence

### Mailchimp vs. Constant Contact
| Feature | Mailchimp | Constant Contact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data/AI** | Advanced Predictive Tools | Basic AI Copywriting |
| **Automation** | Complex Branching | Simple Workflows |
| **Free Plan** | Minimal (Heavily Restricted) | No Free Plan (Trial Only) |
| **E-Commerce** | Deep Native Integrations | Solid Support |
| **Learning Curve** | Steeper (Robust Toolset) | Lower (Intuitive UI) |

*   **Key Advantage:** Mailchimp is the platform of choice for data-driven schools and organizations that want to leverage AI for deep audience personalization and complex automated funnels.
