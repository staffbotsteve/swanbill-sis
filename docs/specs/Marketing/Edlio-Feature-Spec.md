# Edlio Feature Spec — School Website CMS

## Overview
Edlio is a user-friendly, specialized CMS for K-12 schools and districts, known for its focus on accessibility, ease of use, and competitive pricing compared to enterprise systems like Finalsite or Blackbaud. It provides an intuitive, proprietary platform that simplifies website management for non-technical staff.

## Core Features

### 1. Website Builder & CMS
*   **No-Code Interface:** Proprietary CMS designed for easy drag-and-drop editing with pre-built templates.
*   **Accessibility Focus:** Built to meet WCAG 2.1 and Section 508 guidelines. Features include alt-text requirements, automatic closed captions, and an AI-powered accessibility checker that auto-corrects errors.
*   **COPE Functionality:** News, announcements, and calendars update across the website and mobile app simultaneously.

### 2. Communication & Alerts
*   **Edlio Broadcast:** Multi-channel notification system for voice, text, and email in multiple languages.
*   **Edlio Access:** Two-way communication tool for families, including translated messages and digital student IDs.
*   **Mobile App:** Custom-branded app for schools/districts delivering news, events, and push notifications.

### 3. Integrated Tools
*   **Edlio Pay:** Secure online payment processing (OSP by Edlio) for fees, donations, and school stores.
*   **Forms & Newsletters:** Built-in email sender, online form builder, and newsletter tools.
*   **Directories:** Filterable staff and teacher directories.

## Integrations
*   **SIS/LMS:** PowerSchool, Skyward, Aeries, Infinite Campus, and Blackboard.
*   **Calendars/Email:** Google Calendar, Microsoft Office 365, and social media (Facebook/Twitter).
*   **Single Sign-On (SSO):** Supports integration for staff and parent accounts.

## Pricing
*   **Model:** Annual subscription based on enrollment size and school type.
*   **Setup Fee:** One-time cost for custom website design and migration.
*   **Positioning:** Generally marketed as a more cost-effective alternative to Finalsite, aiming to consolidate multiple tools (CMS, mass alerts, payments) into one budget line.

## Pain Points
*   **Feature Depth:** May lack some of the advanced marketing automation and granular enrollment tracking found in Finalsite.
*   **Customization Limits:** While user-friendly, design flexibility can be more restricted than in high-end DXP systems.
*   **Integration Gaps:** Some legacy SIS integrations might not be as seamless as Native-to-SIS systems like Blackbaud.

## Competitive Intelligence

### Edlio vs. Finalsite
| Feature | Edlio | Finalsite |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ease of Use** | High (Simpler Interface) | Moderate (Deeper Learning Curve) |
| **Accessibility** | AI-Powered Auto-Fixer | Built-in Checker (Manual Fixes) |
| **Market Focus** | Strong in Canada/Mexico/US | Global Presence / Enterprise-level |
| **Enrollment** | Integrated Forms/Inquiry | Full Enrollment Lifecycle CRM |
| **Pricing** | Mid-Tier (Competitive) | Premium (Higher TCO) |

*   **Key Advantage:** Edlio's AI-powered accessibility tools and simpler interface make it attractive for districts with smaller marketing teams or those prioritizing compliance over complex marketing funnels.
