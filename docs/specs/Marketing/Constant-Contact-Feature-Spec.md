# Constant Contact Feature Spec — Email Marketing

## Overview
Constant Contact is a versatile, user-friendly email and digital marketing platform suitable for small businesses, nonprofits, and educational institutions. It provides a robust, tiered solution for email automation, list management, and lead generation.

## Core Features

### 1. Email Marketing
*   **Drag-and-Drop Editor:** Easy-to-use builder with AI content recommendations and industry-specific templates.
*   **AI Content Generation:** Generate email copy from prompts and remix emails into social media posts.
*   **A/B Testing:** Subject line testing (Standard and Premium plans only) to optimize open rates.
*   **Campaign Management:** Tools for one-off newsletters, event invitations, and automated welcome series.

### 2. Marketing Automation
*   **Basic Sequences:** Automated welcome emails and birthday/anniversary greetings.
*   **Multi-Step Flows:** Event-based triggers for follow-up emails and nurture paths.
*   **Custom Automation Paths:** Advanced building of workflows based on triggers, conditions, delays, and actions (Premium plan).
*   **SMS Marketing:** Unified platform for sending text and email campaigns (US only, monthly SMS limits apply).

### 3. List Management & Segmentation
*   **Segmentation:** Group contacts by profile fields, engagement level (opens/clicks), or shopping activity (e.g., Shopify/Etsy).
*   **Lead Generation:** AI-powered "Lead Magnet" tool (beta) for surveys and interactive list growth.
*   **Contact Import:** Tools to sync, upload, and organize contacts with custom fields.

### 4. Landing Pages & Social
*   **Landing Page Builder:** Custom, mobile-responsive pages for sign-ups, event registrations, or link-in-bio.
*   **Social Marketing:** Create, schedule, and report on social posts across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.
*   **Social Ads:** Integration for managing social media advertising directly from the dashboard.

## Integrations
*   **E-Commerce:** Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, and Squarespace.
*   **CRMs:** Salesforce, Zoho, and Microsoft Dynamics.
*   **General:** Google/Outlook Calendar, Canva, and Zapier.

## Pricing (2024–2025)
*   **Lite:** Starts at $12/month (500 contacts). Basic email and social tools.
*   **Standard:** Starts at $35/month (500 contacts). Includes A/B testing and contact segmentation.
*   **Premium:** Starts at $80/month (500 contacts). Includes custom automations and advanced SEO/ads.
*   **Nonprofit Discount:** 20% for 6-month prepay; 30% for 12-month prepay.

## Pain Points
*   **Price Scaling:** Costs increase significantly as the contact list grows (usage-based).
*   **Template Rigidity:** While user-friendly, some advanced designers find the editor restrictive compared to Mailchimp.
*   **Plan Limitations:** Key features (A/B testing, custom automation) are locked behind higher-priced tiers.

## Competitive Intelligence

### Constant Contact vs. Mailchimp
| Feature | Constant Contact | Mailchimp |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ease of Use** | Generally considered simpler | More complex UI |
| **Automation** | Strong (User-Friendly Paths) | Advanced (Deep Personalization) |
| **AI Content** | Integrated Copywriting | Broad AI Tools (Predictive) |
| **Social Media** | Integrated Posting/Ads | Robust Multichannel Tools |
| **Support** | Strong Phone/Chat Support | Tiered Support (Chat Only/Phone) |

*   **Key Advantage:** Constant Contact's simpler interface and strong phone support make it ideal for smaller schools or nonprofits with limited marketing staff.
